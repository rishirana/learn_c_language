Continue & Break Statements

It is sometimes desirable to skip some statements inside the loop or terminate the loop immediately without checking the test expression . In such cases , break and continue statements are used.

break keyword

break statement neglect the statement after it and exit compound statement. In th loop and transfer the control outside the loop

break it's sole purpose to passes control out of the compound statement i.e. Loop,Condition,Method or Procedures.
